About Us

Sun Oaks Manufacturing was founded by a group of passionate collectors, with one mission — to craft display stands as unique as each collector’s treasure. We understand the emotional value behind every collectible, which is why our designs range from minimalist wood and metal lines to LED-enhanced features that make your collection truly shine. Whether placed in a commercial space or the comfort of your home, every piece we create is a proud statement of your passion — and a bold extension of your personal style.
